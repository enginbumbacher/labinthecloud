How to's:

Scroll all the way up & left so you see the video in the center of the screen. Above its top-left corner, there is an arrow pointing to the left (see image above). Click on this arrow to make the left sidetab disappear, and click on it again to make it re-appear.

You can make this sidetab disappear by clicking on little arrow in the bookmark on the left, which says "Help" (see image). You can make this sidetab re-appear by clicking on the arrow again. Same with the other sidetabs on the right, with bookmarks like "Modeling".

"Play" - Button to start the video.

"Reset" - Button to set the timer of the video back to zero.

"Normal / Faster Speed" - Button to increase or decrease the replay speed of the video.

Circular Slider - When the video is paused, you can drag the circular slider back and forth to look through the video and set it to the desired times.

The images on the Experiment Tab (see image above) show the programmed light sequence of the corresponding experiment. For example, in the image above, the experiment starts with 100% bright light from the left for 15 seconds, then with 100% bright light from the top for 15 seconds, etc.

1. Click on the "Model" tab on the top left.
2. Set the desired value of the parameters, or choose a model from the dropdown menu.
3. Click on "Simulate Model".

Troubleshooting:

In this case, you haven't simulated the model yet. To do so:
1. Click on the "Model" tab on the top left.
2. Set the desired value of the parameters, or choose a model from the dropdown menu.
3. Click on "Simulate Model".
